Ethical Guidelines for Cybersecurity Professionals

As cybersecurity professionals, we must adhere to ethical principles to ensure we protect data and systems responsibly:

1. Respect for Privacy

Only access information you're authorized to view and handle all data with confidentiality.

2. Do No Harm

Ensure your actions don't cause damage to systems, networks, or people.

3. Maintain Integrity

Be honest about your skills, don't misrepresent findings, and avoid conflicts of interest.

4. Know and Follow Laws

Understand and comply with all relevant laws and regulations in your jurisdiction.
